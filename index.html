Game Rules & Controls
Movement:
- W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys: Move player
- Spacebar: Jump

Combat & Building:
- Left Click: Attack zombies / Mine blocks (All blocks breakable!)
- T Key: Place selected block (Blocks block zombies!)
- 1-9 Keys: Switch hotbar items

Health & Hunger:
- ❤️ Health: 10 total, don't let it reach 0!
- 🍖 Hunger: 10 total, eat zombie meat to restore
- Health regenerates over time (except Extreme mode)

Day & Night:
- Day: Bright sky, normal zombies
- Night: Dark sky, MORE zombies spawn
- Build a shelter to survive nights!
